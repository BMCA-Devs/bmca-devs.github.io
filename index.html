AI Coders

You might be curious about our thoughts on coding AIs—although I should say, this is mostly my personal opinion. I truly believe that AI is now a part of our everyday lives, and the future will be built with it. We’re already living in a time where AI plays a big role, and there’s really no way to avoid it. That’s why I think it’s better to accept this change and learn how to use AI in a smart and responsible way. One quote I find very important is: “AI is a tool to help you, not to replace you.” I believe this is something we should all remember—now and in the future. It’s not about avoiding AI completely, but also not depending on it too much. The key is to find a good balance. When it comes to the best coding AI, in my opinion, GitHub Copilot stands out. I once gave the same coding problem to GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, and CodeGPT. Copilot responded quickly, wrote correct code, and even explained how it worked. The others had logic errors and didn’t offer clear explanations.
